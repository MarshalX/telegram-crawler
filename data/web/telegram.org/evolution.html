The Evolution of Telegram

On this page, we've compiled a Brief History of Telegram. It ended up rather on the 'humongous' side of 'brief' – but that's what you get for never, ever, ever releasing an update that contains only 'bug fixes and minor improvements'.

A (Not So) Brief History of Telegram

This story is written backwards – from the latest update to the primordial summer of 2013. So if you're a fan of chronology, you can jump to the end and scroll up from there. This page gets updated each time a new post hits the Telegram blog.

2025

October 2025

Comments in Group Calls, Notes for Contacts, Suggested Birthdays and More

Participants in group calls and video chats can send comments and reactions directly in the call interface. When adding or editing a Telegram contact, you can include a private note that is only visible to you. Bots support multiple threaded conversations and can stream responses as they are generated. Anyone can report bugs or suggest features via an official mini app – without leaving Telegram.

On Telegram since October 10, 2025:

  • Comments in Group Calls (Group Calls added in 2025, Video Chats added in 2021)
  • Notes for Contacts
  • Suggested Birthdays (Birthdays added in 2024)
  • Threads and Streaming Responses for AI Bots
  • Removing Gift Signatures
  • Displaying Blockchain Gifts
  • Bugs and Suggestions Mini App (Bug and Suggestions Platform added in 2020)

August 2025

Music on Profiles, Stickers Mini App, New Profiles, and More

Audio files from your chats can be added to your profile. The @Stickers mini app allows anyone to create and manage sticker and emoji packs. Users can select a default tab for their profile to show off their gifts or shared stories. Owners of collectible gifts can set dynamic chat themes, based on each gift's unique attributes. Gifts can also be upgraded for other users and show an approximate resale price based on recent sales.

On Telegram since August 31, 2025:

  • Music On Your Profile (Media Player added in 2017)
  • Stickers Mini App (Stickers added in 2015)
  • Default Profile Tab
  • Gift Themes (Chat-Specific Themes added in 2021)
  • Upgrading Gifts for Other Users
  • Gift Value (Gift Marketplace added in 2025)
