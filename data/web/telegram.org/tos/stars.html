Telegram does not process these transactions, therefore, any issues or concerns regarding Star purchases should be addressed to the respective payment processor.

3. Refunds

All sales of Telegram Stars are final and Telegram does not issue refunds for unwanted or unintentional purchases of Stars. Should you decide to delete your account or otherwise lose access to it for any reason, your remaining Star balance will be considered forfeit.

3.1. Disputing Purchases

If a bot or mini app fails to deliver your purchase as advertised and within the agreed-upon timeframe, the respective third-party developer has the ability to refund your Stars at no penalty. Bots should be able to provide payment support if you send them the command /paysupport.

Should the developer refuse to process a legitimate refund following a /paysupport request, you can retrieve the relevant transaction ID from Settings > Your Stars and submit a report here.

Stars you purchased are kept in a personal balance on your account. Once Stars have been added to this balance, you are free to use them to purchase digital goods and services from bots and mini apps, access paid media, donate to content creators or send Telegram gifts. The Telegram platform offers digital goods and services that can be purchased with Telegram Stars, such as Telegram gifts and gift subscriptions to Telegram Premium. Third-party bots and mini apps may also allow you to purchase other digital goods and services with Stars.

If you believe that a bot or mini app is not operating in good faith, make sure you have contacted the bot using the command /paysupport to resolve the issue. If this is unsuccessful, you can submit a report using our bot.

6.5. Sending Messages

In private chats, a user’s contacts are always able to message them feely. Private chats also support additional exceptions, allowing certain users to privately message the user free of charge. Similarly, group members with specific roles, such as admins, can be exempted from messaging fees in group chats.

Users or who enable messaging fees for their chats set the amount of Stars required to send each message. Those users receive 85% of the Stars paid to send each message. Should the user wish to, they may refund the sender of paid messages, returning the full amount of Stars paid to originally send the messages.
