7. Blockchain Integration

If your Mini App integrates functionality related to the generation or distribution of cryptocurrency tokens or blockchain assets, it must exclusively use The Open Network (TON) blockchain for the creation and distribution of said tokens or assets.

7.2. Connecting Wallets

Any Mini App that utilizes functionality related to cryptocurrency wallets must only interface with wallets connected via the TON Connect SDK. This includes, but is not limited to, user authorization and signing of blockchain transactions, as well as the sending and receipt of any cryptocurrency tokens or blockchain assets.

For clarity, this applies both to Mini Apps that directly integrate cryptocurrency wallets and those that indirectly interact with cryptocurrency wallets in any capacity. Using other wallet connection protocols is permitted for the purpose of enabling users to bridge assets from other blockchains.

7.3. Multichain Wallets

Mini Apps that function as multichain wallets, whether they be custodial or non-custodial, are permitted to let users manage assets on other blockchains, provided that such actions are performed directly within the interface of the Mini App. If available, any interactions with other apps and platforms must exclusively be carried out via TON Connect.

For clarity, this includes interactions with other Mini Apps and external apps, as well as embedded catalogs within Mini Apps and external apps.

7.4. Promotion

Mini Apps are prohibited from directly or indirectly promoting cryptocurrency wallets, unless such wallets are based on the TON blockchain, exclusively use TON Connect for interactions with apps, and primarily provide asset management for the TON Blockchain.

Mini Apps are prohibited from directly or indirectly promoting cryptoassets that are not based on TON.

Additionally, Mini Apps must not direct or link users (through any means) to external platforms, apps or websites where cryptoassets not based on TON are promoted or utilized.

For clarity, this includes but is not limited to any content, promotions and newsletters within the mini app itself, as well as its branding (e.g., description, logo, etc.), paid Telegram advertisements and announcements via Telegram channels or communities managed by or officially affiliated with the mini app.

Without limiting the above and Section 6.2., if your mini app issued a TON-based token that is listed on a licensed exchange, then you may direct users to that exchange, provided both your mini app and the exchange comply with all applicable regulations and these Terms.

For specific examples of services that are permitted or not permitted by these terms, reference our blockchain guidelines.

To maintain uninterrupted access for Telegram users to their Mini Apps, developers must ensure compliance with the clauses 7.1–7.4 above by February 1, 2025.
